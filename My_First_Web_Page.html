The Black Keys

ABOUT

TThe Black Keys is an American rock band formed in Akron, Ohio, in 2001. The band consists of Dan Auerbach (guitar, vocals) and Patrick Carney (drums). They are known for their garage rock sound and have released several successful albums, including "Brothers" and "El Camino."

The Black Keys have a rich history and a significant impact on modern rock music.
